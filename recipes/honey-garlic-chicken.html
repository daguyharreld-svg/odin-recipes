Honey Garlic Chicken Bites

Description

This recipe will show how to make honey garlic chicken bites. If you're stumped on what to cook, this is a good starter. It will keep you and your family/friends filled.

Ingredients

For four servings, prepare the following:

Chicken Bites

  • 8 skinless, boneless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon granulated garlic
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil

Sauce

  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 3/2 teaspoons everything bagel seasoning (optional)
