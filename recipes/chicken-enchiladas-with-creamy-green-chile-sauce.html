Chicken Enchiladas with Creamy Green Chile Sauce

Description

This recipe will show how to make chicken enchiladas with a tasty sauce. This recipe is easy to make and can be thrown into an oven. After that, it will be baked and the family/friends can have it.

Ingredients

For six servings, prepare the following:

  • 2 tablespoons or more of vegetable oil
  • 12 corn tortillas
  • 3 cooked boneless skinless chicken breast halves
  • 12 ounces shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 3/4 cup minced onion
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 4oz can chopped green chiles, drained
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F and grease a 9x13 baking dish
  3. Heat two tbsp of oil in a skillet over heat, then fry tortillas
  4. Drain oil from the tortillas on layers of paper towel and keep warm
  5. Divide chicken, 10oz monterey Jack cheese, and onion among the tortillas
  6. Roll up each tortilla and place them seam-side down in the pan
  7. Melt butter in a saucepan over heat, adding flour and whisk until it boils
  8. Add chicken broth and stir until thickened. Mix in sour cream and green chiles. Do not boil
  9. Pour mixture over enchiladas
  10. Bake the pan in an oven for 20 minutes
  11. Place monterey jack cheese and bake for 5 more minutes
  12. Garnish with chopped green onions and cilantro
