Watermelon Lemonade

Description

This recipe will show how to make some cups of watermelon lemonade. It's like lemonade but with the watermelon element. This drink is perfect for the summer.

Ingredients

For six cups of watermelon lemonade, prepare the following:

  • 4 cups cubed watermelon
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 3 cups cold water
  • 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 6 cups ice cubes

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Place watermelon into a blender, cover and puree until smooth. Strain through a mesh
  3. Bring sugar and 1/2 cup water to a boil over medium heat until sugar dissolves
  4. Remove from heat and stir in 3 cups of cold water and lemon juice
  5. Divide the ice into glasses, scoop 2-3 tablespoons of watermelon puree over ice, then top with lemonade
  6. Gently stire before serving
